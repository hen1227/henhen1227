Project Timeline

This is a record of most of the bigger projects I have done in Highschool.

  • Copy Paste Version 1.0

    Late April, 2020

    This was my first app I have ever coded. I made it on xcode with swift 5. Right after finishing I started work on Fish's Tale. A much more ambitious app.

  • Fish's Tale

    Mid May, 2020

    My brother and dad liked to fish. I wasn't a big fan but I occationally came along with them because I enjoyed keeping track of where they caught the most fish.

    After coding the Copy Paste App, I saw how easy it was to save data on the users device. I quickly started on a app that would keep track of where someone had caught the most fish. I had hoped to finished before summer but the project became bigger than I expected. But I was having so much fun I didn't stop adding feature after feature. In total it added up to about 18 thousand lines of code. Because I knew almost nothing about the language, I spent most of my time looking stuff up and rewriting the code I just made. I had finished the app around October but kinda lost interest once the fishing season was over. I mostly focused on school.
